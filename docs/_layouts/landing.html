Chromeless is a free and open-source app for Mac, PC & Linux computers that lets you create a Chromium-based app (also known as site-specific browser, chromeless app, Chrome-based app, Edge-based app) out of any website or web application, effectively turning your favorite web apps into self-contained, distraction-free desktop apps, all powered by your daily web browsers - including:

WebKit (Safari)

  • Mozilla Firefox support is experimental and only available on macOS and Windows.
  • WebKit (Safari) support is only available on macOS.

Your favorite apps now have a permanent home on your Mac and PC as a desktop application that appears in your dock or taskbar. Additionally, every app is isolated from each other, running in its own container - effectively protecting you from cross-site trackers.

Tabbed Apps: Chromeless also lets you create tabbed apps featuring a traditional browser style window - giving advanced users more options to customize workflows.
